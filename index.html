ABOUT DARIO'S CAFE'
"Breakfast - brunch - lunch - afternoon cake Dario’s is owned and run by an authentic Italian couple. We may be small, but our vibe has a real Italian feel that is part coffee bar, part restaurant, part family feast. Join us for breakfast, Roman - style pizza, the best gourmet burgers in the bay, pasta, piadine, daily gastronomic delights, salad, sandwiches, in-house baked cakes, croissants and more. We are fully licensed, with ice-cold beers and wine and of course, Grappa to settle digestion. We offer fast, free WiFi The littles can enjoy outside play area Gift Vouchers available We can make a small function venue available for kid’s birthday parties, adult birthday parties, baby showers, product launches... just ask us!"
